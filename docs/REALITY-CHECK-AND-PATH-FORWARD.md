## The Truth About Software Development

**You cannot rush quality software development.**

- Facebook took years to build
- Instagram started with 1 feature
- Successful platforms iterate

**Your options:**
1. Accept the 8-month timeline
2. Reduce the scope significantly
3. Hire more developers to parallelize

**What you cannot do:**
- Demand 8 months of work be done instantly
- Expect AI to replace months of human development
- Ignore the mathematical reality of development time
